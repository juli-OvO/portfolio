BOID SEQUENCER

Launch Project

This project is an interactive audiovisual sequencer that combines time-based instrument scripts with spatial, event-driven sound generation.

Each element can be toggled or isolated, allowing the system to function as both a compositional tool and an experimental instrument for studying cause and effect in generative sound.

At its core, each instrument follows an independent time-based sequence (e.g. eight-step hi-hat and kick, seven-step piano and bass). On top of this baseline rhythm, boid-based agents act as mobile sequencer elements: when they cross harmonic lines or segments, they trigger additional sound events. Each system, temporal and spatial, can be isolated to study its individual effect, or layered together to produce emergent rhythms.
