Orbit of Desire

A collaborative game project in Brown-RISD Game development club

← back

A sci-fi romance visual novel set aboard an intergalactic government botany research station

Players manage plant growth systems while navigating branching dialogue, affection meters, and multiple narrative endings. Environmental variables (temperature, light, humidity) subtly influence both plant mechanics and character relationships, integrating gameplay systems with story progression.

Character design, line art, coloring by me and my partener

drafts

I worked as the Character Designer and Illustrator for Uba Bobuba, a bubbly herbalist with a delicate constitution and morally ambiguous curiosity.

My contribution included:

  • Designing Uba’s full visual identity (pose variations, emotional states, silhouette clarity)
  • Refining her character arc in collaboration with writers
  • Advocating for tonal nuance, preserving her unsettling edge without flattening her into a caricature
  • Ensuring cohesion between narrative themes and character expression
