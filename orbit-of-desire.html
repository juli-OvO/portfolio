A sci-fi romance visual novel set aboard an intergalactic government botany research station

Players manage plant growth systems while navigating branching dialogue, affection meters, and multiple narrative endings. Environmental variables (temperature, light, humidity) subtly influence both plant mechanics and character relationships, integrating gameplay systems with story progression.

Character design, line art, coloring by me and my partener
